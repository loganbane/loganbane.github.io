Bane Designs Services

Bane Designs is a unique and innovative company that brings the enchanting beauty of the ocean into your living spaces. Specializing in a diverse range of seashells, their collection boasts an exquisite array of shapes, sizes, and colors that are sure to captivate any admirer of marine aesthetics. From the delicate pastel hues of conch shells to the striking patterns of cowrie shells, Bane Designs offers a curated selection that adds a touch of coastal elegance to any setting.

What We Do Best

  • Exquisite seashells sourced from the most pristine beaches around the world.
  • Unique and rare shell varieties that are exclusive to Bane Designs.
  • Eco-friendly practices in harvesting and curating seashells to support marine conservation.
  • Customization options for creating personalized shell-based decor and jewelry.
  • Impeccable craftsmanship that transforms seashells into true works of art.
  • Collaboration with local artisans to promote sustainable livelihoods in coastal communities.
  • Innovative seashell-based designs for home decor that blend seamlessly with any style.
  • Interactive workshops and tutorials on seashell art for enthusiasts of all ages.
