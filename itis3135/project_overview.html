Project Overview:

Application and Purpose: The website aims to serve as an interactive platform for a volleyball team, facilitating communication, sharing information about the team, and engaging with fans and the community.

Intended Users: Volleyball enthusiasts, fans, potential sponsors, and community members interested in team activities.

Overview of Content: The website will feature sections such as home, about us, players, schedule, photo libary, and contact, providing relevant information for each category.
