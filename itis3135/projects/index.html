Fall Guys

Mission Statement: Friends united in volleyball, where camaraderie fuels our game. We play with passion, support each other relentlessly, and celebrate every win as a testament to our bond.

"Fall Guys" is a volleyball team comprised of friends who compete against other friend-based teams in a semi-competitive environment. Despite not representing a college or formal organization, Fall Guys take their matches seriously, striving for victory while fostering camaraderie and sportsmanship on and off the court. Their name reflects their lighthearted approach to the game, where they aim to have fun and enjoy the thrill of competition together.
